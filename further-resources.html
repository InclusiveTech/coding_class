Further resources

Foundations and events

Code Club is an organisation that runs free coding clubs for the 9-13 year old across the UK and globally.

Code Club is part of Raspberry Pi Foundation, a UK-based charity that works to put the power of computing and digital making into the hands of people all over the world.

Online resources

W3Schools is an educational website that offers online web tutorials and examples. It offers tutorials on HTML, CSS, JavaScript, as well as other programming languages.
